Cast Library Session Notes

Supporting Cast

Hubert

The Ghost's first encountered Hubert on a scorcher of a day, brutally hot as the crew to went investigate the haunted mansion on the cliffside. As he lay against a tree, passed out in the sun, the Sea Ghosts roused him. With a flash of gold and a wiff of rum Hubert was employed for the first time in his life. Since then he has joined the Ghosts on many an adventure, and has proven himself worthy of membership in the crew as a non-combatant. He currently serves as Spymaster for Tony Montazuza, Renowned Thief and Quickmage, and also Groundskeeper to the Cliffside Estate owned by the Sea Ghosts.

Anders Solmor (Deceased)

The very first ally of the Sea Ghosts, responsible for the introduction of the party and the first person to assign them to a quest. More than just an employer, Anders was a friend to Tony Montazuza, Alfonso, and Davion. The Ghosts successfully recovered Anders' mothers' belongings from the Emperor of the Waves while encountering a Sahaugin and supernatural threat. Their success in that mission was ultimately pivotal in securing further mercenary work and developing further connections with high ranking city officials. Unfortunately, Anders was not safe in his own home. As it turned out, Anders' butler, Skarren Waverunner, was the head of a cult in the region known as 'The Scarlet Imperium'. Unbeknownst to anyone but himself, Skarren Waverunner accompanied his Master Anders and the Sea Ghosts on a mission in the Gulf of Nerina after it was uncovered that another member of Anders' household, Egart the accountant, was a member of the Scarlet Imperium when he killed Uvohs Copperbranch. During this mission, Skarren was discovered to be the 'Noble' and when confronted, slit Anders throat.

Wolgar Windrune

Wolgar Windrune. Entire sagas could be written about the exploits of the captain of the Red Tide. His most recent expeditions saw him ferrying a group of adventurers to a shipwreck in the Gulf of Nerina, the mental toll upon his crew was significant. So much so that shortly after their return there was a mutiny. And so, Wolgar Windrune found himself without a ship or a crew. All was not lost however, shortly after the death of Uvohs Copperbranch the Sea Ghosts found themselves in need of an extra gun. Wolgar joined the party as they broke bread with Lizardfolk, tamed Thousand Teeth the Devourer, and faced off with Gulpa'Gor. In his final act of brilliance, Wolgar stole the crew's skiff and sailed to the Tower of Zamar where he led the Ghosts to find Xolec's new haven as well as their newest member, Lyrax Copperbranch. Wolgar found a sense of purpose and belonging with Xolec that had been missing since the mutiny and he elected to stay at the tower.

Eliander Fireborn

A member of the city council and a staunch ally of order, the Sea Ghosts, and the Crown of Sidon. Employer of the Sea Ghosts shortly after their introduction during a council meeting, Eliander Fireborn is responsible for a large portion of the crew's journey thus-far. He has provided the coin and authority with which the Sea Ghosts have acted during their larger quest to protect Emerald Bay. Deeply invested in uncovering the mystery surrounding the Scarlet Imperium, Eliander enacted marshal law shortly after the deaths of Manistrad Copperlocks and Uvohs Copperbranch. Successful in large part, he was able to discover the identity of the Bull and have him hanged for conspiracy and treason. Upon their return, Eliander has granted the Ghosts impunity in their mission for justice.

Oceanus

Member of a Sea Elf tribe off the coast of Uskarn. Oceanus first introduced himself to the crew as they made combat during a journey. He spoke of great danger for not only the underwater species but the land dwellers as well. Somehow, Davion was able to understand the Sea Elf dialect and intrepret for the crew. Oceanus was welcomed for a time and accompanied the Sea Ghosts as they brokered an alliance with the Lizardfolk, Merfolk, Locathah and a number of other aquatic races. Once that was completed he returned to his native village to prepare for the coming conflict with the Sahaugin.

Xolec

Imprisoned underneath the abandoned section of Emerald Bay, Crabber's Cove, the crew were granted an audience with Xolec after a chance meeting with the gnome Dimble. Upon entering the prison, the Ghosts noticed holy magic afoot and Wolgar Windrune took it upon himself to free the magically chained vampire. After his release, he immediately gorged himself on the corpse of Vito the Fixer before introducing himself. An alliance was formed and Xolec agreed to help gather information on the Scarlet Imperium in exchange for his freedom. He also delivered the body of Uvohs Copperbranch safely to Stonekeep. Since then, Xolec has made hearth in the Tower of Zamar, where he introduced the crew to Lyrax Copperbranch and set them on a quest to retrieve a 1500lb statue of Saint Cuthbert.

Uriel Plume

Priest of Agwe, Uriel Plume is Emerald Bay's resident holy man. Quite philosophical, but uselful nevertheless. Uriel was able to remove the aging curse that was wrought upon Tony and Lyrax after their encounter with the spectres. Lyrax' intention was to stiff Uriel of the gold for performing the reversal, but Alfonso being the kind soul he is, offered up his own 450 gold pieces.

Gellan Primewater

A member of the Emerald Bay city council, Gellan Primewater held a position of immense power within the political hierarchy of Emerald Bay. A close ally and debtor to the Solmor family, he is known to throw extravagant parties at his compound in the city. It is a well known secret that the Primewater household is a front for a massive smuggling operation. It is under this pretense that Gellan has been arrested and imprisoned by Eliander Fireborn after he enacted marshal law. There is evidence that Gellan and Eda Oweland may have in the past, and perhaps presently, engaged in relations.

Eda Oweland

Member of the City Council and representative of the elven population, Eda Oweland is a well respect influence in the bay. Much about her is unknown outside of her professional relations and rumored romance with Gellan Primewater. Her house holds a number of traps including animated carpets.

Thousand Teeth the Devourer

A massive, nearly prehistoric, crocodilian monster, Thousand Teeth the Devourer was once regarded as a god amongst the Lizardfolk in the Dunwater. Upon encountering him, Alfonso was able to sooth his violent tendencies and uncover the source of his rage. Where his eye used to rest in the socket lay a swirling black glass-like orb. Alfonso was able to remove the orb and in doing so experienced a vision of a deep sea rift with a seemingly infinite number of hostile creatures emerging from it. These creatures were making their way towards and island with an obelisk in the center. Tony and Davion also touched the orb and experienced the vision. The Lizardfolk were grateful for the neutralization of Thousand Teeth in a humane way.

Lykos

Encountered when the Ghosts were searching for the statue of Saint Cuthbert, Lykos and his sister Aella were quick allies in a strange and unpredictable situation. It was discovered that they were searching for the statue as well. It was their father's crowning achievement and they desired to bring it home. The party allowed them to take it and in return Lykos let Tony use it's scrying ability. Tony was able to focus his energies to scry on the Noble and uncover his identity.

Aella

Aella is the sister to Lykos and the commander of the automaton Lucky. A mage of distinguished power she was quick to lend Lucky in support of the party after they uncovered the identity of the Noble and engaged him in combat.

Lucky

Automaton. Power On -> Initializing Main Sequence -> Initiate Combat Sequence -> Engaging Enemies -> Delivering Fatal Blows.

Zarra the Herbalist

Sells herbs and potions at the Tower of Zamar. Little beyond that is known of the Drow.

Minister Sauriv

Counsel and advisor to Queen Saphara of the Lizardfolk, Sauriv was hesitant at first to welcome the employ of the Sea Ghosts, but after seeing their prowess and results, recognized their value and earnest determination to provide aid to the Lizardfolk.

Queen Saphara

Queen of the Lizardfolk, Saphara. An ally of the Sea Ghosts after forming an alliance with both them and Emerald Bay through the efforts of Anders Solmor. Queen Saphara agreed to the alliance if the party would deal with the threat of Thousand Teeth the Devourer. After vanquishing the threat and returing to the fortress, Saphara ordered a Sahuagin executed in front of them for planting the orb upon Thousand Teeth. Afterwards, she welcomed the party to stay, drink, and celebrate their victory.

Dimble

A strange little gnome discovered by Tony and Davion after emerging from the Emerald Bay sewers. If Dimble owned a home, which he most certainly did not, one might say that the lights are on, but no one is home. Nonetheless, Dimble was instrumental in the Sea Ghosts learning more about the Scarlet Imperium and fostering an introduction with the vampire Xolec.

Neekle Burnwater

Goblin bartender at the Kraken's Call.

Kaladek

The Wizard Kaladek, denizen of the Tower of Zamar, well known in the powerful circles of Emerald Bay. Kaladek was previously operating the tower before it came under new management, he doesn't seem to mind the change. When he last spoke with the party he implored them to see what they could do to spare the life of Gellan Primewater in exchange for information on the whereabouts of the Portrait of Constantori.

Manistrad Copperlocks (Deceased)

A member of the City Council and representative for the dwarves. Perhaps a bit of stiff, Manistrad was all about business and she showed it in the first interaction in the council chambers. Who can say what truly sealed her fate? None but the hanged Guther Stoutmaw and the other members of the Scarlet Imperium can answer that.

Mango

A creation of the late Uvohs Copperbranch, Mango is a loyal ally of the Sea Ghosts and his reappearance in the fog of the gulf's coastal waters was a surprising but welcomed development. Mango was able to successful help Tony navigate to the Tower of Zamar, where the part was introduced to Uvohs' brother, Lyrax.

Tobeax

Goblin wizard and purveyor of the finer things, Tobeax can be found in the goblin district of Emerald Bay where he sells a variety of magical and non-magical goods.

Adelaide

As a housekeeper in the Solmor household, is Adelaide accessory to Uvohs' murder? Almost certainly. Was she coerced into doing so? Perhaps, but that is unknown by the Sea Ghosts at the current time. Adelaide's fate is yet to be determined.
